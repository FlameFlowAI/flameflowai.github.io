Why I blog?
Created 2015-06-07| Updated 2020-03-17|Sentiment
Word count:672|Reading time: 4 min
| Post View:

Under normal circumstances, before people do anything, they will think about why to do it, or whether it is worth doing. Before I decided to start blogging, I also thought: Why should I blog?

To blog or not to blog; that is a question.

The answer I think about is that because I like to write, blogging allows me to exercise and enjoy writing.

I like to write about the environment in which I grew up. My family is a bit remote, so when I was young, most of the time I played alone. I did n’t say much every day, which made me a more introverted personality and not Good at dialogue and communication.

Fortunately, I learned to write in school, learned to write a diary, learned to use words to communicate with others, to make friends with words, and even to use my words to communicate with my first love …

Writing really brings me a lot of happiness, and I really like writing. And unfortunately, although I like to write, I am not good at writing. I often could n’t finish the 800-word composition in the exam room before I submitted the paper. Sometimes I wanted to write, but I did n’t know what to write .

I like to write, but writing is difficult for me, which makes me very painful. In order to eliminate this pain, I must practice writing so that I can write more characters smoothly.

The reason I’m not good at writing is not enough, and the reason I don’t write enough is because I’m afraid I can’t write well and be ridiculed. I must be brave to face my shortcomings, and overcome this kind of fear of writing. This is how I persuade myself, I said to myself:

-For those who write better than you, he may think that I write better and have a sense of accomplishment than this one
-For those who write worse than you, he may think that this person writes better than me and is still trying to practice writing, and I should also work harder
-For those who have the same level of writing, he no longer feels alone when he sees someone trying to write as hard as himself.

Regardless of whether it is good or bad, you don’t have to worry about it, because the text you write can be beneficial to others and to yourself.
No one is perfect. Some things do n’t seem real because of imperfection, and what is real is beauty! So enjoy writing sincerely!

whatever is worth doing at all is worth doing well .—— Whatever is worth doing is worth doing well.

Experience tells me that if I want to do a good job, the best way is to do it consistently, trial and error bravely, to find the wrong, to correct it, to keep improving, practice makes perfect, write a sword in ten years, writing also requires constant practice

I choose to write on a blog because blogs can be shared and sharing is also a pleasure. I just want to continue to practice writing in the process of blogging and improve my writing level.

If you are lucky, you can get feedback from readers, or play a role in attracting readers and making yourself better, so blogging is a good place to write.

Writing because you like, writing is a kind of enjoyment, why not do it? I am very glad to have made this decision to make blogging a part of my life, to know myself through writing, and to keep improving!
